Onboarding Flow

This demo shows what happens when a user completes the onboarding carousel and clicks "I'm Ready".

What happens:

  • Grainy white overlay appears
  • Squiggly arrow points to top-right (extension icon)
  • Arrow pulsates and squiggles
  • Text hint appears
  • Click anywhere to dismiss

Click the extension icon to get started!
